Polyhack - Fall 2016
Mismatch
A mobile-friendly website designed to help colorblind or other visually impaired persons pick out outfits. We used an API to give us the main colors in a photo, which we then used in our algorithm to give the user feedback on the colors of the clothing items, the saturation of the colors, and how much the colors contrasted.
